Exploratory Data Analysis: Electric Vehicles

Dec 2024 – Jan 2025 · University of New Haven

NumPy pandas Matplotlib Power BI Data Cleaning EDA

Overview

Collaborated in a team of three to analyze electric vehicle usage in Washington State. Performed data cleaning, visualization, and statistical analysis to surface insights such as Tesla’s market dominance and Seattle’s leading EV ownership. Built interactive Power BI dashboards with slicers, maps, and time-series views.

Highlights

  • Processed/cleaned dataset in Python (missing values, renaming, validation).
  • Analyzed correlations between features and visualized results via charts.
  • Delivered a Power BI dashboard enabling dynamic exploration and city-level insights.
